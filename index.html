Curriculum

What You'll Learn With Me

From zero knowledge to confident trader — here's my structured learning path.

How Markets Work
BSE, NSE, Sensex, Nifty, market hours, order types, and how stocks are bought and sold.
Candlestick Patterns
Doji, Hammer, Engulfing, Morning Star and 20+ patterns to predict price movements.
Chart Reading
Support, resistance, trendlines, breakouts, and volume analysis for best entry/exit points.
Technical Indicators
RSI, MACD, Moving Averages, Bollinger Bands — how to use them to confirm trade setups.
Fundamental Analysis
Evaluate company health, read balance sheets, and choose long-term winners.
Trading Psychology
Control fear and greed, maintain discipline, develop a winning trader mindset.
Risk Management
Position sizing, stop-loss strategies, and capital preservation — the #1 skill.
Portfolio Building
Diversify across sectors, balance risk vs reward, build a growing portfolio.
